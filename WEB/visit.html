How Do I Find My Book?

Our books are organized by genre.
From there, there's no further organization.
Browse through a possible genre of interest,
read the characteristics on the cover,
and check out your books at the front office!

Please do not peek by ripping off cover paper,
it ruins all the fun and takes time for us to rewrap.
Please wait to unwrap once checking out with the book.

If you're looking for a specific book:
Our experience is to be paired with a random book.
We will try to help,
but you may need to visit another local library.
