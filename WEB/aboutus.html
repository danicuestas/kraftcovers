About Us

Kraft Covers Library

Our library is established by the local government of Tampa. We are a nonprofit corporation that is open to the public. However, we are not just your every day library.

What makes us different?

We make sure our readers don't judge our books by their covers... literally! We wrap all of our books in kraft paper and other recycled materials (such as newspaper, leftover giftwraps, and more).
All our books have 5 bulletpointed lists handwritten onto their kraft paper covers that provide a glimpse into the book.

We list characteristics such as:
  • Horror
  • Romance
  • Has a movie made after it
  • 7.8 Star Rating on Good Reads App
  • Part of a series
  • Personal Note: One of my favorites, it's a book you can read over and over and never get tired of!
